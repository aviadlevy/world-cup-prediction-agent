### Verdict

- **EV pick: Spain (~16%)** — top of every model, but expect 3–4 colleagues on it and a brutal left-half bracket (France in the semi).
- **Differentiation pick (recommended): England (~11%)** — best probability-per-expected-picker. Cleanest injury sheet, easiest group, right-half bracket whose rivals all carry flags (Brazil decimated, Argentina aging, Portugal over-priced vs Opta). An England win is likely yours alone; a Spain win is split 4 ways.

## 2. Top Scorer / Golden Boot (מלך השערים)

FIFA tiebreaks to a single winner (goals → assists → fewest minutes), so optimize purely for "finishes #1 in goals".

| Harry Kane | England | 12.5% | 13.0% |
| Mikel Oyarzabal | Spain | 7.5% | 8.0% |
| Erling Haaland | Norway | 5.5% | 7.0% |
| Michael Olise | France | 4.7% | 4.0% |
| Cristiano Ronaldo | Portugal | 4.5% | 5.0% |
| Julián Álvarez | Argentina | 4.4% | 4.0% |

| Raphinha | Brazil | 3.7% | 4.0% |
| Lamine Yamal | Spain | 3.4% | 4.0% |

### Structural notes (top candidates)

- **Mbappé**: penalty taker, but knee injury (2 months out this season) + thigh injury, blanked in last friendly, and the hardest contender group (Senegal, Norway).
- **Kane**: 61-goal Bayern season, fully fit, penalties, Panama in the group, England projected deep.
- **Oyarzabal**: Spain's penalty taker and starting 9 for the tournament favorite; **softest group of any contender (Cape Verde, Saudi Arabia + Uruguay)**; scored in 6 straight internationals; Spain projects 7–8 games.
- **Haaland**: monster form (16 goals in qualifying) but shares Group I with France → likely 2nd place → brutal bracket side, ~4–5 games. Avoid.
- **Messi/Ronaldo**: nostalgia picks — low volume, Messi carrying a thigh issue. Avoid.

### Crowd estimate

Mbappé 4–6 picks, Kane 3–4, Messi+Ronaldo 2–4, Haaland 2–3, Yamal 1–2. **Oyarzabal: ~0–1 despite being #3 in every market** — near-zero name recognition among casual pickers. This is the pool's biggest inefficiency.

### Verdict

- **EV pick: Harry Kane (~13%)** — arguably ahead of Mbappé on fundamentals (fitness, form, group), but split 3–4 ways if he wins.
- **Differentiation pick (recommended): Mikel Oyarzabal (~7.5%)** — third-highest true probability, near-solo equity. In a top-2 pool of 15–20, a ~7.5% solo pick dominates a ~14% pick split five ways.
