# CONTEXT — World Cup 2026 Office Pool (Tiko)

Glossary of domain terms for an office World Cup 2026 pool run on the Tiko app.

## Terms

**Pool** — A private office group on Tiko (colleagues at work). ~100 NIS buy-in per participant. Payout: ≤20 participants → 70%/30% (1st/2nd); >20 → 70%/20%/10%.

**Bull (בול)** — An exact-score prediction that matches the final result precisely. Pays the higher point value for the stage.

**Direction (כיוון)** — A prediction that matches only the outcome (home win / draw / away win), not the exact score. Pays the lower point value for the stage.

**Regular-time result** — The result that determines scoring is the score at the end of regular time (90 min + stoppage). Extra time and penalties do not count.

**Per-game prediction** — An exact-score pick entered in Tiko before each match kicks off. One per match, locked at kickoff.

**Futures** — The two one-time picks made upfront: Winning Team (קבוצה מנצחת) and Top Scorer (מלך השערים). Each pays 10 points if correct.

**Stage point values** (Bull / Direction):

| Stage | Bull | Direction |
|---|---|---|
| Group stage | 3 | 1 |
| Round of 32 | 3 | 1 |
| Round of 16 | 4 | 2 |
| Quarter-final | 6 | 3 |
| Semi-final | 8 | 4 |
| Third place | 10 | 5 |
| Final | 10 | 5 |
| Winning Team (future) | 10 | — |
| Top Scorer (future) | 10 | — |

**Challenges (אתגרים)** — A Tiko feature marked "active" in this pool. Details unknown.

**Pure-EV strategy** — The pool stance: every prediction (per-game and futures) maximizes raw probability of being correct. Other participants' picks are ignored entirely — no contrarian or differentiation logic.
